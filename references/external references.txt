External References
====================

1. Churn Rate Industry Benchmark (Telecommunications)
   Focus Digital. "Average Churn Rate by Industry."
   https://focus-digital.co/average-churn-rate-by-industry/
   Used in: Section 1.1 (Business Understanding - Context), to benchmark the company's
   historical churn rate (26.69%) against the telecom industry average (~21.5-22%).

2. Customer Retention vs. Acquisition Cost Research
   Harvard Business Review; Frederick Reichheld (Bain & Company).
   Used in: Section 1.1 (Business Understanding - Context), regarding the finding that
   acquiring a new customer typically costs 5-25x more than retaining an existing one.

3. Telecom Customer Retention Program Success Rate (Save Rate) Case Study
   Qualfon. "Telecom Customer Retention: 160% Improvement."
   https://www.qualfon.com/resources/case-studies/telecom-customer-retention-160-percent-improvement/
   Used in: Section 8.3 (Business Calculation), as the basis for the retention program
   success rate assumption range (10%-40%) used in the business impact simulation.

4. Churn Model Monitoring & Retraining Threshold
   UK Data Services. "Customer Churn Prediction Guide | Predictive Analytics for UK
   Businesses." Published 8 June 2025.
   https://ukdataservices.co.uk/blog/articles/predictive-analytics-customer-churn
   Used in: Section 1.4 (Business Understanding - Analytical Approach) and Section 9.2
   (Recommendation - Model), as the basis for the model retraining trigger: ROC-AUC
   dropping below the 0.75 threshold (churn-model-specific benchmark).

Notes:
- All figures derived from these external sources are illustrative benchmarks used to
  contextualize assumptions (e.g., ARPU, retention success rate, monitoring threshold).
  They should be validated against internal company data (Finance/CRM) before being
  used as the basis for final business decisions, as noted throughout Section 1.6 and
  Section 8.3.
